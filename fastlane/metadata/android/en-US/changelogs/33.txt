This version introduces a major redesign of the home page.

Key changes include:
- Replaced the bottom navigation bar with a FloatingActionButton for
  quick access to the redesigned navigation sheet.
- The AppBar now features dedicated icons for search and bookmarks,
  improving accessibility to these core features.
- The navigation sheet has been refactored for a more intuitive
  user experience, including a simplified debouncing mechanism.
- Various styling adjustments to the surah header and basmala for
  improved aesthetics.
